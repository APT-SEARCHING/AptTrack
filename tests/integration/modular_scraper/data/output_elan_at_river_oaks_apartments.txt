Élan at River Oaks
Élan at River Oaks Apartments in San Jose
Substance meets style at Élan at River Oaks, a North San Jose luxury apartment community. Designed with the busy professional in mind, Élan offers residents amenities that make mingling easy. Just minutes from downtown San Jose—the heart of Silicon Valley—Élan is ideal for those who want to be close to fine dining, retail, and endless entertainment.
Upscale Apartments and Extraordinary Amenities
Connected to River Oaks Retail Center featuring Starbucks and other convenient dining and shopping options, residents of Élan at River Oaks are also just minutes from jogs along the Guadalupe River and a short rideshare from downtown San Jose. Experience exceptional service from Elan's onsite team that is top rated by residents with
530+ five star reviews
on Google.
Apartment interiors feature granite countertops, raised-panel cabinetry, hardwood-style flooring, wood-burning fireplaces and designer lighting. Attached patios are perfect for sipping coffee and catching up on emails in the morning. 

Residents in the mood for mingling can enjoy a night of gaming at the resident lounge, or catch up with a neighbor at a poolside cabana. Hit the weights or jump on a treadmill in a state-of-the-art fitness center, play a few sets on the tennis courts, or swim in the community’s lap pool.

Located off Montague Expressway, easy access to Interstate 880 and 101 makes for simple travel throughout Silicon Valley and the peninsula. And if you need to fly the friendly skies, the airport is less than 15 minutes away.
