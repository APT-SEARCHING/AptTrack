Pet Policy
Diridon West is a pet-friendly community! Pets may be permitted with an additional fee; dogs are restricted by breed. Dog breeds not permitted are Staffordshire Bull Terriers, Dobermans, Rottweillers, Chow Chows, Pit Bull Terriers and any mixes thereof. Resident must sign a separate Animal Addendum and provide a copy of the pet's veterinarian records, including weight, and a picture of the pet, prior to approval. No more than two pets allowed per apartment. Pet fees are on a per pet basis. Pet policies and guidelines are strictly enforced.
Enjoy Up To One Month Free
Move in during the month August to receive up to one month free on all apartment homes. Contact our leasing team for details.
