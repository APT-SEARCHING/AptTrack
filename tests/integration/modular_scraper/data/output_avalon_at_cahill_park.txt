The Avalon Experience
Live Modern. Live Up.
Make Yourself at Home
We believe elevating where you live is all about finding a way to blend it seamlessly with how you live. We go to great lengths designing amenities and choosing locations that put everything within reach. Where you live, is where you come alive.  Avalon at Cahill Park in San Jose is just steps from the historic downtown district and has generously appointed and spaciously designed one-, two- and three-bedroom apartments for rent that boast high ceilings, gourmet kitchens, private patios and other quality details found throughout every floor plan. And now with our Furnished+ program, apartment living is easier than ever before. Appreciate the stylish furnishings and enjoy the convenience of pre-installed Wi-Fi and cable TV, flexible lease-terms, and utilities that are included in your rent. With Furnished+, you can step right into the life you want to live, without all the hassle. Upgrade any home to Furnished+ and enjoy the convenience of turnkey living. Upgrade any home to Furnished+ and enjoy the convenience of turnkey living. Plus, our technology package, AvalonConnect, offers high-speed and common area WiFi, digital resident services including the ability to submit service requests and pay your rent online, Amazon package lockers, and more. Avalon at Cahill Park offers a slew of luxurious features and amenities designed to make the lives of our residents as comfortable as possible. Living in our San Jose apartments also means you are offered tremendous community amenities including a swimming pool, spa, resident lounge, and state-of-the-art fitness center. Our on-site restaurants and retail includes Whole Foods Market for added convenience. We also offer easy access to highways 880, 280, 87, and 101, making commuting a breeze.
